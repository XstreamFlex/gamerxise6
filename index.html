About GamerXise

GamerXise is a gamer fitness company that creates exercise programs that can be done while playing your favorite video games with Xbox or PlayStation controller in hand. GamerXise delivers full body, strength and cardio workouts from your living room, with no additional equipment required. GamerXise is designed to improve both body and mind.

GamerXise not only improves users' health and fitness, but also their reaction time, brain-body relationship, and can have dramatic improvements on game skill. GamerXise users have reported fat loss, muscle growth, improved focus, improved hormones, improved mood, and they achieved all this while playing their favorite games.
