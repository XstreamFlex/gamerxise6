About GamerXise6

GamerXise is a gamer fitness company that creates exercise programs that can be done while playing your favorite video games with Xbox or PlayStation controller in hand. Delivering full body, strength and cardio workouts from your living room, GamerXise is designed to improve both body and mind.

Our programs not only improve users' health and fitness but also their reaction time, brain-body relationship, and can have dramatic improvements on game skill. GamerXise users have reported fat loss, muscle growth, improved focus, improved hormones, improved mood, and they achieved all this while playing their favorite games.
